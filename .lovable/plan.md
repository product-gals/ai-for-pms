

# Revised PM × AI Resource Hub Plan

Two key changes based on your feedback:

## 1. Brand Alignment with justfractional.co

Pulling colors directly from the site:

- **Primary:** Deep purple/indigo (`#4A4494` to `#6B5CE7` gradient range) -- used in hero backgrounds and headings
- **Accent/CTA:** Coral/salmon (`#FF8A75`) -- used for buttons and call-to-action elements
- **Text:** Dark navy (`#1A1A2E`) for headings, medium slate for body
- **Background:** Light lavender-white (`#F5F3FF`) with white card surfaces
- **Section label text:** Muted purple (`#7C6DC7`)
- **Stats/numbers:** Slate-purple bold treatment matching the site's metric style

The hero will use a subtle purple gradient similar to justfractional.co's top section. Cards will be clean white with soft shadows. CTAs in coral.

## 2. Community-First, Not Creator-Centric

Shifting the tone from "Meagan's hub" to "the hub PMs share with each other":

- **Hero copy:** "The free resource hub for PMs navigating AI" (no personal byline in the hero)
- **Attribution:** A small "Curated by Meagan Glenn" note in the footer, not front-and-center
- **Content framing:** Resources are presented as community picks, not personal recommendations. Cards say things like "Popular with PMs" or "Community favorite" rather than "Meagan's pick"
- **Practitioner Spotlights:** Stays -- this section naturally highlights others
- **Tool Reviews & Frameworks:** Written neutrally, surfacing the best resources from across the web (articles from Lenny's Newsletter, SVPG, Reforge, etc.) rather than only original content
- **"Start Here" section:** Curated reading lists and external guides, not just original articles

## Everything Else Stays the Same

The page structure (Homepage, Start Here, AI Tools, Frameworks, What's Changing, Practitioners, Go Deeper), client-side search, newsletter capture placeholder, responsive design, and static content data approach all remain as previously planned.

3. Homepage with hero + 6 section cards
4. All 6 content section pages with placeholder resources
